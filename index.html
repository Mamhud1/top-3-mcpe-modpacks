Top 3 modpacks for MCPE

3. Hexxit

Hexxit is a Technic modpack created by the Technic Team. It is designed to
encourageexploration through its many dungeons, with some inspiration
from Dungeons & Dragons. Hexxit is possibly the most popular (by downloads)
Technic modpack,
and one of most popular modpacks in general, with near 8 million downloads.

2. Better on Bedrock

Better on Bedrock is a remarkable Minecraft Bedrock Add-On
designed to enhance the gameplay experience of the vanilla game.
Its main objective is to improve various aspects of the game
by introducing new content and transforming the old one.

1. RLCraft

RLCraft is one of the most challenging modpack ever produced for Minecraft,
and it is finally on bedrock. There are a plethora of features that make it more tough,
such as new animals that do massive damage.
